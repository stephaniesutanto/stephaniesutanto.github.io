About

Stephanie is a recent graduate of the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) holding a bachelor's degree in Business-Economics. As an Economics Sharpe Fellow and a UCLA Venture Capital Fund/Startup UCLA Fellow, her accomplishments include having co-founded UCLA's first undergraduate entrepreneurship organization, led efforts in establishing UCLA's first entrepreneurship minor as well as managed a student-run accelerator program.

Asides from her strange adoration for pulling cheesy pickup lines on her friends and random unsuspecting strangers, she has a deep interest in digital health and firmly believes in its value in enabling global citizens today receive better and consistent quality care.
